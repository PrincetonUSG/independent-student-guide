Local
Discounts &
Free Food

Looking for a way to save some cash? Be sure to check out the USG’s twice annual restaurant week for discounts at local restaurants.

Also. free food flows freely at Princeton; one student actually tried to live off of free food for a week! On the Point event listing you can search for events for free food. Also be sure to read through the Weekly Events email, as well as your department emails for events with catered food.

Also, make sure to check out the listing of free food events on Point for current free food opportunities!

Restaurants



Visit the Point directory of restaurants in Princeton, and you can also find some menus from local restaurants here. If you have a car, you can also explore additional options up and down route 1.
