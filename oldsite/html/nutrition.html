Tips on Maintaining a Healthy and Balanced Diet
for Independents and Everyone Else

All these sites provide useful advice to maintain a healthy diet as a student cook.

USDA: Food Pyramid and Nutrition advice

Food labels: Guidance on how to understand and use the nutrition facts panel on food labels

Dietitian.com: Q&A, Healthy Body Calculator, Tips to Prevent Weight Gain, etc.

Navigator: A rating guide to hundreds of nutrition websites, with links to hot topics, weight management, general nutrition, & women's/men's nutrition.

About.com, Home Cooking: USDA approved article on college health, including links to recipe & fitness sites

The Dining Services Nutrition Page: info and monthly articles, plus contact information for the campus dietitician. It's especially helpful for anyone who works out.
