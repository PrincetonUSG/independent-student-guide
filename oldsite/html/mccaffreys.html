McCaffrey's Free Delivery Service!

The USG has negotiated an exciting new food delivery service that promises to be much more long-lasting than past efforts.

With a minimum order of $25, McCaffrey's Princeton will deliver food free of charge to your dorm room. You can phone, fax, or e-mail your order.

Place your order by 4:00 p.m. for a guaranteed next day delivery between 12:00 noon and 4:00 p.m. No specific time can be requested.

Please take a look at the Princeton McCaffrey's. In addition to their specialty services listed on the website, they offer all basic groceries at competitive prices.
