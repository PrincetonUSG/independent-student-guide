The Independent Student Guide

For the purposes of this guide, an "Independent Student" is any Princeton student who is:

1. a junior or senior and not affiliated with an eating club
or
2. looking to eat or cook independently of the University system

Note: this is not the Housing Department's definition of an "independent student" -- see: Independent Draw Options (on the Housing Department's Room Draw Guide).

The Independent Student Guide was compiled to:

  • Assist with the many challenges and adjustments faced by all Independent Students
  • Provide all students with a multitude of useful information about living in Princeton
  • Help students considering going independent make informed decisions

We hope this guide has accomplished all these goals and more! Please frequently take advantage of the services offered on this page.
